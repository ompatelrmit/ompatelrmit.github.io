NPC of Zombie AI

NPC of Zombie AI was created using Unreal Engine 5 with Blueprint and Animation Blueprint. This AI is fully functional in a multiplayer environment, where it detects and relentlessly attacks the nearest player until they are defeated. When no players are nearby, the zombie roams the map, and once it reaches a player, it plays a hit animation, enhancing the gameplay experience. And All of the Features can be found on the youtube video.

Zombie AI Spawn System

Zombie AI Spawn System was created using Unreal Engine 5 and Blueprint. This system spawns a group of zombies when the player triggers specific locations. The trigger and spawn locations are connected but separate, adding an element of surprise. Once spawned, the zombies perform their usual AI tasks. The map also tracks the number of zombies spawned, providing an engaging and dynamic gameplay experience.
